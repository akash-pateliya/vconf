About V-Conf

V-Conf is India's leading car search venture that helps users to buy car of their choice and within their budget. This website and app carry rich automotive content such as expert reviews, detailed specs and prices, comparisons as well as videos and pictures of all car brands and models available in India.

V-Conf has launched many innovative features to ensure that users get an immersive experience of the car model before visiting a dealer's showroom. This includes a tool which gives a feel of experiencing the interior and exterior view of car.

Our vision is to construct a complete ecosystem for consumers and car manufacturers, dealers and related businesses such that consumers have easy and complete access to not only buying and selling cars, but also manage their entire ownership experience.
